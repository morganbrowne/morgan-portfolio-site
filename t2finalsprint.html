This React application is designed for an online shopping experience, with a focus on browsing products, managing a shopping cart, and completing purchases. App Component: Manages the layout and routing of the application, using ShoppingCartProvider to share cart data and Router for navigation. It sets up routes for viewing products, the shopping cart, product details, and the checkout page. ProductList Component: Displays a list of products fetched from an API. Users can click on a product to view its details. ProductDetail Component: Shows detailed information about a specific product and allows users to add it to their cart. ShoppingCart Component: Provides an overview of the items in the cart, calculates costs, and allows users to proceed to checkout. Checkout Component: Facilitates the finalization of the purchase by collecting billing information and confirming the order. Together, these components create a cohesive shopping experience, from product discovery to purchase completion.
